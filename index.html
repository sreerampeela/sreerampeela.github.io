About Me

I am a Computational Biologist with over 5 years of postdoctoral experience, bridging the gap between clinical microbiology and high-performance genomics. Currently working at IIIT-Delhi, I specialize in integrative cancer omics, pathogen surveillance, and building scalable machine learning workflows. My unique background (BDS → Ph.D. Microbiology → Computational Postdoc) allows me to understand biological data from the bench to the server.
